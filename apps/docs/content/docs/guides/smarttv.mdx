# CicadaVPN on Smart TVs

To secure your Smart TV, you'll need to connect it to a VPN-enabled router.

## Step 1: Set Up CicadaVPN on Your Router

1. Follow our [Router Setup Guide](#).
2. Once the VPN is running on your router, all devices connected to the router, including your Smart TV, will be secured.

## Step 2: Connect Your Smart TV to VPN-Enabled Router

1. Go to **Settings** on your Smart TV.
2. Navigate to **Network Settings** and select your VPN-enabled Wi-Fi network.
3. Connect to the network.

Your Smart TV is now secured with CicadaVPN.

For any issues, contact [support@cicadavpn.com](mailto:support@cicadavpn.com).
