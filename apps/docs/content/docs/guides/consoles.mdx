# CicadaVPN on Consoles (PS5, Xbox)

To secure your gaming consoles, you need to connect them to a VPN-enabled router.

## Step 1: Set Up CicadaVPN on Your Router

1. Follow the instructions in our [Router Setup Guide](#).
2. Once the VPN is active on your router, all devices, including your PS5 or Xbox, will be protected.
