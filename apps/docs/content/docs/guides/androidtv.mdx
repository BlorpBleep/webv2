# CicadaVPN on Android TV

You can install CicadaVPN directly on your Android TV by downloading the Android app.

## Step 1: Download and Install CicadaVPN on Android TV

1. Open the **Google Play Store** on your Android TV.
2. Search for **CicadaVPN**.
3. Select the CicadaVPN app and click **Install**.

## Step 2: Log In and Connect

1. Open the CicadaVPN app after installation.
2. Log in with your account or redeem an activation code.
3. Choose a server location and tap **Connect** to secure your connection.

Your Android TV is now protected by CicadaVPN.
