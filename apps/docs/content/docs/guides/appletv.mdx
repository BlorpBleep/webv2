# CicadaVPN on Apple TV

CicadaVPN does not have a dedicated Apple TV app, but you can still secure your Apple TV by connecting it to a VPN-enabled router.

## Step 1: Set Up CicadaVPN on Your Router

1. Follow the instructions to set up **CicadaVPN on your router**. See the [Router Setup Guide](#) for details.
2. Once the VPN is active on your router, all devices connected to that router, including your Apple TV, will be protected.

## Step 2: Connect Apple TV to VPN-Enabled Router

1. Go to **Settings** on your Apple TV.
2. Select **Network**, then **Wi-Fi**.
3. Choose your VPN-enabled routerâ€™s network from the list and connect.

Your Apple TV will now route its internet traffic securely through CicadaVPN.
