# CicadaVPN on Fire TV

You can install CicadaVPN directly on your Fire TV device.

## Step 1: Download and Install CicadaVPN on Fire TV

1. Open the **Amazon Appstore** on your Fire TV.
2. Search for **CicadaVPN**.
3. Select the CicadaVPN app and click **Download**.

## Step 2: Log In and Connect

1. Launch the CicadaVPN app after installation.
2. Log in with your CicadaVPN account or redeem a code.
3. Choose your desired server location and click **Connect**.

Your Fire TV is now protected with CicadaVPN.

Need help? Contact [support@cicadavpn.com](mailto:support@cicadavpn.com).
